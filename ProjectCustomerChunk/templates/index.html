How It Works

1

Upload Your Data

Navigate to the upload page and select your customer churn CSV file. The system supports the Telco Customer Churn dataset format with features like tenure, monthly charges, contract type, and more.

2

Automatic Model Training

Once uploaded, the system automatically cleans your data, handles missing values, encodes categorical features, applies PCA for dimensionality reduction, and trains a Logistic Regression model. You'll see the accuracy and feature reduction metrics.

3

Get Predictions

Visit the prediction page, fill in customer details (gender, services, billing info), and instantly receive churn probability. Use these insights to identify at-risk customers and take proactive retention measures.
